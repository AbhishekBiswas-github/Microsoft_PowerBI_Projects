## üìä Project Overview

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of **Apple's global sales, inventory management, and product performance** from 2020 to 2024. The dashboard offers a 360-degree view of business operations, ranging from financial contribution and store performance to warranty claims and geographical trends.

The goal of this dashboard is to assist stakeholders in identifying revenue drivers, optimizing inventory, and monitoring warranty claim rates across different markets and product categories.

---

## üîç Key Insights & Metrics

Based on the dataset analyzed, the following high-level metrics were established:

- **Total Revenue Generated:** $6.17 Billion
- **Total Units Sold:** 5.72 Million
- **Global Warranty Claim Rate:** 52.44%
- **Top Revenue Product:** Apple Music ($125.45M)
- **Top Sales Volume Product:** HomePod mini (23K units)
- **Top Market:** United States (~20% of global revenue)

## üìë Dashboard Pages & Features

The report allows users to navigate through **8 specialized analytic views**:

### 1. **Overview**
   - High-level KPIs: Total Revenue, Claim Rate, Total Claims, and Unique Products.
   - Snapshots of the best-performing products by Revenue and Sales volume.
   - Yearly revenue trends at a glance.

### 2. **Sales Trends**
   - Monthly sales analysis with Year-over-Year (YoY) growth percentages.
   - Revenue trends broken down by fiscal years (2020-2024).
   - Identification of peak sales periods (Max Sales Date: August 10, 2022).

### 3. **Category Performance Analysis**
   - Revenue distribution across major categories (Tablets, Accessories, Smartphones, etc.).
   - Comparison of **Claim Rates** per category (e.g., Accessories showing a high claim volume).
   - Trend lines for sales vs. sold items over time.

### 4. **Store Performance Analysis**
   - **Top 10 Stores by Revenue:** (e.g., Apple Chadstone, Apple Covent Garden).
   - **Bottom 10 Stores by Sales:** Identifying underperforming locations.
   - Detailed store-wise comparison tables.

### 5. **Geographical Performance**
   - Interactive Map visualization showing global footprint.
   - Country-wise and City-wise revenue breakdown.
   - US, Australia, and China identified as the top 3 contributing regions.

### 6. **Product Performance Deep Dive**
   - Granular analysis of individual product SKUs.
   - Top 5 and Bottom 5 products based on revenue generation.
   - Sales timeline since product launch.

### 7. **Warranty and Claim Analysis**
   - Deep dive into post-sales support metrics.
   - Analysis of Claim Status (Rejected, In Progress, Pending, Completed).
   - Correlation between specific countries and high claim rates.

### 8. **Financial Contribution Analysis**
   - **Waterfall Chart:** Visualizing how each category contributes to the total $6.17bn revenue.
   - Decomposition Tree: Breaking down revenue from Category > Product > Sub-variant.
